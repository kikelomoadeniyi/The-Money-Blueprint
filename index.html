The six types

Which pattern is
running your finances?

Every money story has a pattern underneath it. Most people are a blend of two types, but one tends to run the show. Does any of these sound familiar?

🏛️

The Architect

"I've built something real. But I know the next level requires a different strategy."

Intentional, consistent, and genuinely in control. The Architect has done the work most people haven't, and they know it.

🦁

The Builder

"I'm disciplined. But I'm terrified of losing what I've built."

Strong with structure and saving, but the fear underneath makes it hard to enjoy or invest freely.

🐘

The Spender

"I earn well. I have nothing to show for it."

Works hard, earns consistently, and somehow ends every month with roughly the same balance as before.

🦒

The Giver

"I take care of everyone. Except myself."

The person family calls when things go wrong. Generous to the point of self-sacrifice and quietly exhausted by it.

🐢

The Avoider

"I want to get it right. So I don't move at all."

Researches everything. Acts on nothing. The fear of a wrong decision costs more than any bad decision ever would.

🐆

The Chaser

"I'm always moving. I never seem to arrive."

High energy, big ambitions, constantly pivoting. Earns well in good months but nothing sticks.
